
#### Q-1 Weather Observation Station 6

```bash

Query the list of CITY names starting with vowels (i.e., a, e, i, o, or u) from STATION. Your result cannot contain duplicates.

SELECT DISTINCT CITY
FROM STATION
WHERE REGEXP_LIKE(CITY, '^[AEIOU]');

or 

SELECT DISTINCT CITY
FROM STATION
WHERE CITY LIKE 'A%'
   OR CITY LIKE 'E%'
   OR CITY LIKE 'I%'
   OR CITY LIKE 'O%'
   OR CITY LIKE 'U%';

```

#### Q-2 Weather Observation Station 7

```bash

Query the list of CITY names ending with vowels (a, e, i, o, u) from STATION. Your result cannot contain duplicates.

SELECT DISTINCT CITY FROM STATION
WHERE lcase(CITY) LIKE '%a'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE '%e'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE '%i'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE '%o'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE '%u'
ORDER BY CITY;

```

#### Q-3 Weather Observation Station 8 

Query the list of CITY names from STATION which have vowels (i.e., a, e, i, o, and u) as both their first and last characters. Your result cannot contain duplicates.

```bash

SELECT DISTINCT CITY FROM STATION
WHERE 
(lcase(CITY) LIKE 'a%'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE 'e%'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE 'i%'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE 'o%'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE 'u%')
AND
(lcase(CITY) LIKE '%a'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE '%e'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE '%i'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE '%o'
OR lcase(CITY) LIKE '%u')

ORDER BY CITY;

```
